Membership

Be The Change and Enroll

Students

  • Be over twenty years of age.
  • Enjoy full civil rights and not have been convicted of a felony or a crime involving moral turpitude.
  • Accept the association’s bylaws and commit to fulfilling its objectives.
  • Hold at least 5 years of work in an academic, professional, or applied capacity in the field of information technology or other relevant technical fields.
Free

Professional Members

  • Be over twenty years of age.
  • Enjoy full civil rights and not have been convicted of a felony or a crime involving moral turpitude.
  • Accept the association’s bylaws and commit to fulfilling its objectives.
  • Hold at least 5 years of work in an academic, professional, or applied capacity in the field of information technology or other relevant technical fields.
$100 /year

Apply for membership

To apply, the applicant must clearly state their desire to join the association and attach the registration form, a copy of their civil status record, and any documents proving their qualifications. The administrative board will review the application and issue a decision. The decision is final and not subject to appeal.
